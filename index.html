Key finding: The series shows a clear structural break in month 12–13 (post-COVID recovery), making the data highly non-stationary. With only 24 months of history, Naive performs best — the series stabilises into a plateau where the last observed value is the strongest predictor. Exponential Smoothing's negative BIAS (−8.4%) signals systematic overforecast → excess stock risk in a real supply chain context.
